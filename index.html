Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan a document?

Tap the + button on the home screen. Your camera will open automatically. Point it at the document — the app will detect the edges and scan it. Tap the shutter button when ready.

How do I export as PDF?

After scanning, tap the document in the list to open the preview. Tap Share PDF to share via AirDrop, Mail, Messages, or save to Files.

Is this app really free?

Yes. Scanbit is completely free. No subscription, no in-app purchase, no account required. The app is supported by a small banner ad.

Where are my scans stored?

All scans are stored locally on your device. No data is uploaded to any server.

How do I delete a scan?

Swipe left on the scan in the list and tap Delete.

How do I rename a scan?

Swipe left on the scan and tap Rename.

よくある質問（日本語）

書類をスキャンするには？

ホーム画面の + ボタンをタップするとカメラが起動します。書類にかざすと自動でエッジを検出し、シャッターボタンでスキャンできます。

PDFとして書き出すには？

スキャン後、リストからドキュメントをタップしてプレビューを開き、Share PDF をタップしてください。AirDrop・メール・ファイルへの保存が可能です。

本当に無料ですか？

はい、完全無料です。サブスク・課金・アカウント登録は一切不要です。バナー広告のみで運営しています。

スキャンデータはどこに保存されますか？

すべてのデータはお使いのデバイス内に保存されます。外部サーバーへのアップロードは行いません。

スキャンを削除するには？

リスト上でスワイプして Delete をタップしてください。

Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Data We Collect

Scanbit does not collect any personal information directly. All scanned documents are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server.

Advertising

Scanbit displays banner ads powered by Google AdMob. Google may collect and use certain information to show relevant ads, including your device's advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID). You will be asked for consent before ads are personalized (EU/EEA users).

For more information on how Google uses data, please refer to:
Google Privacy Policy
How Google AdMob uses data

Camera Access

Scanbit requests access to your camera solely for the purpose of scanning documents. Camera data is processed on-device and is never transmitted externally.

Photo Library Access

Scanbit may request access to your photo library only when you choose to save a scan to your photos. No photos are accessed without your explicit action.
